About Me

I have graduated from highschool, and am heading to college as a Computer Science major. I've programmed since 2008, with guidance from my brothers. Otherwise, I am self-taught in Computer Science. From the simple games I wrote in the third grade, to the hackathon winning team project that I led, my skill has been accelerating to where it is now. I am able to work on large projects and am writing amateur but complete games.

Of the mainstream languages, I use Java the most, but I can also use Javascript and Python to about the same degree. I am also looking to pick up C# or C++ soon. For game development, the type of project I spend most time on, I am also picking up Godot Engine. I had been using LibGDX, a game oriented graphics library in Java.
