About

I am a high school senior. I love programming and being obnoxiously "good" at competitive games. I am self-taught in Computer Science, and I have privately produced clones of some games. Currently, I use Java, but I can be flexible and switch to another language.
To make games, I have been using LibGDX, a game oriented graphics framework in Java. I am getting around to using the Godot Engine, and I plan to make something sometime soon.
